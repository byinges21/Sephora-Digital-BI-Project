# Dashboard Specification (Tableau/Power BI)

## Audience
- Director, Digital Analytics
- Product Managers (site/app)
- Digital Merchandising and Marketing partners

## Page 1: Executive KPI Overview
### KPIs
- Sessions
- Orders
- Revenue
- Session-to-order rate
- Average order value (AOV)
- Revenue per session (RPS)

### Visuals
- KPI cards with vs. prior period delta
- Revenue trend by day
- Revenue share by channel
- Conversion rate by channel

### Filters
- Date range
- Channel
- Traffic source
- Campaign
- Personalized vs non-personalized

## Page 2: Channel & Funnel Performance
### Visuals
- Funnel chart by channel: view -> cart -> checkout -> order
- Drop-off heatmap by channel and stage
- Traffic source x channel matrix with conversion and revenue

### Business questions answered
- Where are the largest leaks in the funnel?
- Which channel has the greatest upside if drop-off is reduced?

## Page 3: Personalization Impact
### Visuals
- Personalized vs non-personalized KPI comparison
- Uplift chart: session-to-order rate and RPS uplift by channel
- Distribution chart for order volume by experience type

### Business questions answered
- Is personalization driving measurable commercial impact?
- Which channels should get more personalization investment?

## Page 4: Opportunity Backlog
### Table columns
- Opportunity
- Metric impacted
- Current baseline
- Target
- Estimated impact
- Effort (S/M/L)
- Owner
- Status

### Seed opportunities
- Improve mobile web checkout speed and simplify payment options
- Increase app recommendation module coverage to more categories
- Refine paid social landing pages to align with campaign intent

# Dashboard Specification

## Canonical KPI Definitions
- `session_to_order_rate = orders / sessions`
- `view_to_cart_rate = add_to_cart / product_views`
- `cart_to_checkout_rate = checkout_starts / add_to_cart`
- `checkout_to_order_rate = orders / checkout_starts`
- `revenue_per_session = revenue / sessions`

## Audience
- Digital Analytics leadership
- Product managers (site + app)
- Marketing and Merchandising partners

## Page 1: Executive KPI Overview
- KPI cards: sessions, orders, revenue, session-to-order rate, AOV, revenue per session.
- Trend line: daily revenue and daily session-to-order rate.
- Channel comparison: revenue and revenue/session by channel.

## Page 2: Funnel Performance
- Funnel by channel: product views -> add to cart -> checkout starts -> orders.
- Stage conversion bars: view-to-cart, cart-to-checkout, checkout-to-order.
- Drop-off spotlight: highlight largest leakage stage by channel.
- Channel x source table: sessions, orders, conversion, revenue.

## Page 3: Personalization Impact
- KPI comparison cards: personalized vs non-personalized.
- Channel split chart: uplift in conversion and revenue/session by channel.
- Revenue contribution chart by experience type.

## Common Filters
- Date range
- Channel
- Traffic source
- Campaign
- Personalization flag
